How to play!

In this game, you'll have to solve a mysterious murder, and then answer trivia-style questions to see if you really know who did it! You can do this by reading through the case files that the police have gathered and with your own insights see into the heart of what really occured! When you feel like you've figured out what happened, click the "Question Time" button to start the Trivia Game!
